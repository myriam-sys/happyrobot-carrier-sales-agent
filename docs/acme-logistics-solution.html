Executive Summary

Acme Logistics is positioned to automate the majority of inbound carrier sales calls — load matching, real-time carrier verification, and rate negotiation — without adding headcount. This document outlines HappyRobot’s solution for your inbound carrier operations: a fully deployed system that handles carrier inquiries around the clock, enforces rate discipline on every call, and gives your operations team real-time visibility through a live dashboard enriched by an independent AI quality layer.

The Problem

Repetitive calls are consuming your best people

A significant share of inbound carrier calls follow a completely predictable pattern: a carrier asks what loads are available, a dispatcher checks the board, quotes a rate, and either books or loses the load. That loop runs dozens to hundreds of times per day — and every one of those calls is a poor use of a skilled dispatcher.

The result is experienced dispatchers spending their time on calls that require no judgment — leaving less bandwidth for the negotiations that actually matter, and zero coverage after business hours.

Solution

A voice AI agent that handles every carrier call, end to end

The HappyRobot agent handles every step of the inbound carrier sales process automatically. Each call is verified, matched, negotiated, and enriched without human intervention — freeing your team to focus on exceptions and high-value decisions.

Real-Time Carrier Verification via FMCSA

Before any rate is discussed, the agent checks the carrier’s MC number against live FMCSA records via a US residential proxy — confirming active operating authority and insurance in real time. Carriers who do not meet requirements are declined immediately and professionally, before any load information is shared.

Load Matching

The agent queries the live loadboard filtered by equipment type and origin, and presents the best available load with full details — rate, pickup date, origin, destination, and mileage.

Rate Negotiation with Enforced Floor

The agent opens at the target rate and follows a configurable negotiation strategy. A hard floor is set at 90% of the posted rate (rate × 0.90) — enforced on every call, no exceptions. The agent either confirms a booking or closes the call cleanly when the floor is reached.

Automatic Logging & Post-Call AI Enrichment

Every call is logged in real time with outcome, rates offered, negotiation rounds, and carrier identity. Immediately after the call, two independent AI layers run: AI Extract generates a structured negotiation summary from the transcript, and AI Classify scores carrier sentiment. This enriched data surfaces in the dashboard within seconds.

AI Quality Validation

An independent AI layer cross-validates the agent’s self-reported call data against the actual transcript. The Sentiment Agreement Rate measures how consistently the agent’s reported outcomes match the post-call AI analysis — giving your team confidence in every data point surfaced.

Operations Dashboard

Your team sees everything, without listening to a single call

The live operations dashboard updates after every call. Your operations team sees booking rates, sentiment trends, top lanes, and revenue in real time — no manual reporting, no delayed summaries. All data is enriched post-call by AI Extract and AI Classify, producing structured summaries and validated sentiment scores.

Technical Architecture

Built to integrate, not replace

The HappyRobot system connects to your existing loadboard via a documented REST API. The backend is hosted and secured by API key — no custom infrastructure required on your side. Deployment typically completes within a single business day.

Layer Component Role
Voice AI HappyRobot Platform Manages call flow, turn-taking, escalation, and post-call AI Extract + AI Classify
Backend API FastAPI (Python) on Railway /verify-carrier, /loads, /calls/log, /calls/enrich, /dashboard/metrics — secured by API key
Carrier Data FMCSA Query Central via US Proxy Real-time authority and insurance verification through a residential US proxy — live, not mocked
