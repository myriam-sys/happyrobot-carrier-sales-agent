Executive Summary

Acme Logistics can automate the majority of inbound carrier sales calls — load matching, carrier verification, and rate negotiation — without adding headcount. This document presents a production-ready system built on HappyRobot that handles carrier inquiries around the clock, enforces rate discipline automatically, and delivers real-time operational visibility to your team.

The Problem

Repetitive calls are consuming your best people

A significant share of inbound carrier calls follow a predictable pattern: a carrier asks what loads are available, a dispatcher checks the board, quotes a rate, and either books or loses the load. That loop runs dozens to hundreds of times per day.

The result is experienced dispatchers spending their time on calls that require no judgment — leaving less bandwidth for the negotiations that actually matter, and zero coverage after business hours.

Solution

A voice AI agent that runs the full carrier call flow

The HappyRobot agent — deployed and tested end-to-end — handles every step of the inbound carrier sales process automatically.

Carrier Verification

Before any rate is discussed, the agent checks the carrier's MC number against FMCSA records in real time — confirming active operating authority and insurance. Ineligible carriers are declined immediately and professionally.

Load Matching

The agent queries the live loadboard filtered by equipment type and origin, and presents the best available load with full details — rate, pickup date, origin, destination, and mileage.

Rate Negotiation

The agent opens at the target rate, follows a configurable negotiation strategy with a hard floor, and either confirms a booking or closes the call cleanly. Rate discipline is enforced on every call — no exceptions.

Logging and Analysis

Every call is recorded with outcome, sentiment, rates offered, and negotiation rounds. An independent AI layer validates the agent's self-reported data against the actual transcript. Your team sees the full picture in real time.

Operations Dashboard

Your team sees everything, without listening to a single call

The live operations dashboard updates after every call. Booking rates, sentiment trends, top lanes, and revenue are visible in real time — no manual reporting, no delayed summaries.
